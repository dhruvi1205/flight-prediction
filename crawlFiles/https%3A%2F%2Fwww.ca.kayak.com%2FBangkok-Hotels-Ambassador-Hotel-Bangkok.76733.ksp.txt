Freebies This stay includes Wi-Fi and Parking for free The Ambassador Bangkok Hotel is located on Sukhumvit 11 Road in Bangkok, Thailand. The hotel is 300 feet from Nana BTS Skytrain Station and a mile from the Queen Sirikit National Convention Center. The 4-star establishment is less than 15 miles from both Suvarnabhumi International Airport and Don Muang Airport. It offers 760 non-smoking guestrooms and suites near many bars and upscale restaurants. There are several rooms and suites to choose from ranging from simple to luxurious. The main wing offers standard rooms, family rooms, and the Amtel Suite. The tower wing boasts superior rooms, deluxe rooms, privilege rooms, superior suites, and privilege suites. Rooms come with digital televisions, tea and coffee making facilities, international telephone dialing, and a personal electronic safe. Complimentary high-speed wireless Internet, hairdryers, and private bathrooms with bath amenities are provided. Suites include a lounge area with a flat-screen television, an executive writing desk, and a leather chair. Am Café Restaurant is a casual dining restaurant offering native and international dishes. Sushi Bar offers more than 70 Japanese dishes, including sashimi, tempura, sushi, and Japanese rolls. For Chinese food, Hong Teh Chinese Restaurant provides a banquet or a la carte options. Espresso 171 and Espresso 11 both offer pastries and coffee. The Bloom Restaurant is the place to go for fusion cuisine. This hotel has a swimming pool, as well as business and internet services. There's a spa, and a fitness center with treatment options like massage, body therapy, facial treatments, bath therapy, and treatment packages.
