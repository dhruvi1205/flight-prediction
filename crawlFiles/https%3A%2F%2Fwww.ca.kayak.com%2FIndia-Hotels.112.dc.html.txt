Average nightly price per month Average nightly price Top tips for finding the perfect India hotel When exploring the different regions of India, it’s hard to avoid a stay in New Delhi, the capital of India. To make the most of your stay in Delhi, you could book a suite at the luxury hotel The Taj Mahal Hotel; it has a wonderful location, no matter what the purpose of your trip is. It is close to a lot of historic landmarks and will put you just a short taxi ride from all major commercial businesses, if you are in town for a meeting or for shopping. At The Lalit New Delhi, you are in the heart of the different shopping experiences Delhi has to offer. Chandani Chowk is one of the oldest and busiest street markets in India; here, you can immerse yourself in an authentic shopping experience. The hotel is opposite a major shopping mall that carries all the top brands, or you can pick up some traditional mementoes at Dilli Haat, with its beautiful silks and spices just around the corner. When moving through India on business, you are bound to come across the silicon valley of India: Bangalore! There are a wide range of hotel stays available in this diverse area. If you are quickly passing through on a budget, check out Grand Choice Hotel, situated close to a mall and the train station, with a 24-hour reception desk and free wifi to get meeting preparation done with no hassle. To unwind after a long day in the boardroom, check out the rooftop bar and lounge of Shangri-La Hotel. This high rise hotel is in an area of Bangalore known as the garden city and boasts beautiful views while being in the thick of the business centre. If it is an all-night party you are after, you need to strap on your dancing shoes and hit Mumbai. Most of the nightclubs and bars are found close to the airport, making ITC Maratha in Mumbai a good choice for you. If you’re looking for a cheap hotel in India, you should consider visiting during the low season. You'll find cheaper accommodations in India in June and May. Hotel room prices vary depending on many factors but you’ll most likely find the best hotel deals in India if you stay on a Sunday. The opposite is true for, Friday, which is usually the most expensive day. Book at least 46 days before your stay begins to get the best price for your India accommodation.
