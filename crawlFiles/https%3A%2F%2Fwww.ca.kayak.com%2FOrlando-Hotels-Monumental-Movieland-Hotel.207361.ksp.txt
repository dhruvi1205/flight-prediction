Freebies This stay includes Wi-Fi and Parking for free The Monumental Movieland Hotel is located just a few miles from International Drive, offering free onsite parking and a jacuzzi for guest use. Plus, the hotel is just minutes from plenty of shopping and dining, and close to all of the theme parks Orlando is known for. Universal Studios, the Disney family of parks, and SeaWorld Orlando are all within a short drive of this hotel. Madame Tussauds and Ripley’s Believe It Or Not! Museum are also nearby. Ambitious guests can even walk to the theme parks, which are about 30 minutes on foot. The hotel offers complimentary WiFi in all guest rooms and public areas and has a total of 261 guest rooms and suites available. The Monumental Movieland Hotel features an outdoor pool and lounge area for guest use, as well as an onsite restaurant. There is a fitness center and a 24-hour business center at the hotel, which also has an arcade and game room for guest enjoyment. The hotel offers a meeting room for small events and meetings, as well as self-serve laundry facilities. There is a 24-hour front desk that provides limo and Town Car service, as well as concierge services. The guest rooms at this hotel all include flat-screen TVs, in-room coffee and tea service, blackout curtains, complimentary toiletries, and modern bathrooms. Complimentary breakfast is provided to all guests at this hotel, which is just 25 minutes from Orlando International Airport. Select guest rooms offer microwaves and refrigerators, and there are a limited number of adjoining rooms available for families and groups. The hotel also offers daily housekeeping.
