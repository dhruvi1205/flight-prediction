It is also the capital city of the country which means that it is rich in history, arts and culture. If you're looking to enjoy the nightclub scene in Manila, you'll want to stay in the commercial hub. Bonifacio Global City is close to hotspots like XYLO at the Palace (a fancy nightclub) and many trendy restaurants. Some hotels in this neighborhood include the Grand Hyatt Manila and F1 Hotel Manila. The Philippines hosts some amazing festivals. Many feature music, dancing, lots of food, and vibrant costumes. One of the biggest and most attended festivals is Sinulog, which is held in the city of Cebu in the month of January. If you book a room at the OYO 476 Hedda Hotel or the One Central Hotel, you'll be within walking distance of the festival. If you're visiting the Philippines for a honeymoon or a romantic vacation, you should get away to one of their picturesque and quiet islands such as Boracay or Palawan Island. On these islands, there are several beachfront hotels with an array of luxurious amenities such as a 24-hour concierge, bicycle rental, laundry service, and onsite dining. This includes the Henann Prime Beach Resort and the Astoria Current in Boracay, and the Seashore and Mangrove Resort in Palawan. Explore the history and effects of the Mayon Volcano with a stay in or close to Darago or Legazpi City. Hotels closest to the volcano include the Mayon Lodging House and the Casa Bicolandia Suites. This neighborhood is also about two miles away from other popular tourist attractions including the Liberty Bell Philippines and Albay Park & Wildlife. If you’re looking for a cheap hotel in the Philippines, you should consider visiting during the low season. You'll find cheaper accommodations in the Philippines in September and August. Hotel room prices vary depending on many factors but you’ll most likely find the best hotel deals in the Philippines if you stay on a Thursday. The opposite is true for, Monday, which is usually the most expensive day. Book at least 28 days before your stay begins to get the best price for your the Philippines accommodation.
